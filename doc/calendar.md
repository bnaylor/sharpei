# Calendar View

The Calendar View provides a monthly grid to help you visualize your tasks and deadlines.

## Switching to Calendar View

Use the view toggle in the toolbar to switch between **List** and **Calendar** modes.

## Navigation

When the Calendar View is active, navigation controls appear in the toolbar:
- **< (Left Arrow)**: View the previous month.
- **> (Right Arrow)**: View the next month.
- **Today**: Quickly jump back to the current month.

The current month and year are displayed prominently in the toolbar.

## Interacting with Tasks

### Visualization
- Tasks with a due date automatically appear in their respective day cell.
- Tasks are color-coded by priority:
    - **Red**: High Priority (P0)
    - **Green**: Normal Priority (P1)
    - **Blue**: Low Priority (P2)
- Completed tasks are displayed with a strike-through and reduced opacity.

### Editing Tasks
To edit a task seen in the calendar:
1.  **Click the task** within its day cell.
2.  Sharpei will automatically switch back to **List View**.
3.  The selected task will be **auto-expanded**, allowing you to immediately edit its description, date, priority, tags, or subtasks.

## Behavior Notes

- **Filtering**: The calendar respects your current category selection and search queries. If you have a search filter active, only matching tasks with due dates will appear in the calendar.
- **No Due Date**: Tasks without a due date are not visible in the Calendar View.
